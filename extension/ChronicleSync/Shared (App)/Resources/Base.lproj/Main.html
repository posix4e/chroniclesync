ChronicleSync

Sync your browsing history across devices

Enable the Safari Extension

  1. Open the Settings app on your device
  2. Scroll down and tap on Safari
  3. Tap on Extensions
  4. Find and enable ChronicleSync
  5. Tap on ChronicleSync and ensure all permissions are enabled

Using the Extension

  1. Open Safari
  2. Tap the AA button in the address bar
  3. Select ChronicleSync from the menu
  4. Enter your Client ID in the popup

You can turn on ChronicleSync's extension in Safari Extensions preferences.
